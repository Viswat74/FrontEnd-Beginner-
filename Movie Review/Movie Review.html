Saripodhaa Sanivaaram is a Telugu romantic action entertainer movie directed by Vivek Athreya. The movie casts Nani and Priyanka Mohan in the main lead roles along with SJ Surya, Abhirami, and many others have seen in supporting roles. The music was composed by Jakes Bejoy while the cinematography was done by Murali G and it is edited by Karthika Srinivas R. The film is produced by D. V. V. Danayya under DVV Entertainment banner.
